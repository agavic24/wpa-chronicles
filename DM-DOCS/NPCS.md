# NPCS

    

    ## Viktor Mazan, the Lycan Lord
    Neutral Good (Peace / Nature Domains)
    A werewolf lord who rules over Shatterstone in Brokenstone Vale, Viktor Mazan aids his fellow werewolves and other lycans in the Feywilds eke out a peaceful existence after a years long war. Viktor does not allow travelers to remain long and sends them off again as quickly as possible. Not everyone wants Mazan’s new civilization, and rumors have begun to circulate that factions in the upper mountains plot to destroy the werewolf lord and resume the war against the fey once more.
    
    ## Lord Calenon Thray
    Ruler of Shinaelestra, the Fading City, a city located in the Feywild. He's an eladrin ranger, some who claim to be the greatest ranger to ever live.

    ## Maiden of the Moon
    An archfey with a special hatred for lycanthropes. Her silver sword is the doom of any werebeast that crosses her path outside the Brokenstone Vale.

    ## The Queen of Nightmare Fairies
    A relatively insignificant Archfey in the court of the Unseelie Fey. The queen acquired Skjolna for Thrumbolg and cursed her into 
    becoming the Bheur Hag, Mother Snæbjörg. Here agreement with Thrumbolg is that she will provide Fomorians access to a stable Fey Gate near an entrance to the Feydark, which they can use to mount their kidnapping raids into the Underdark, in exchange for protection for her agents, a race of corrupted pixies, who will travel with them. The Queen's corrupted pixies infest mortal beings with terrifying dreams using Nightmare Toxin, a hallucinogenic poison, which expends psychic energy that the pixies collect and bring back to the Queen for her personal feasting.
