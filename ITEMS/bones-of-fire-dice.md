The Bones of Fire Dice are magical divination dice that Darias uses to cast the Augury spell.  When activated, the burst into small magical flames and after about a minute, the flames subside, leaving glowing red omens on the dice faces.

Four possible outcomes
- WEAL (good results)
- WOE (bad results)
- WEAL WOE (mix results)
- BLANK (indifference - neither good or bad)
