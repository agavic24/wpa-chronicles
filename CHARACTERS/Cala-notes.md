# Calabaza De Peligro Ratto Aka."Cala"

## PC Notes

Male, Fairy, Rogue, Arcane Trickster: 

It was a foggy moonlit dream of a night when the chanced encounter produced Calabaza. A roaming Ratkin was bedazzled by the rusted glitter of dried blood on the wings of a flesh-eating Fey, while the Fey appreciated the sizable teeth and claws on the Ratkin. The gods chanced to look away for the evening's events and thus Cala came into being.

A mercurial creature, Calabaza De Peligro  was raised in a dual custody situation. His mother being a member of the Autumn Fey court, he was with his Fey relatives for fall/winter and his Ratkin relatives during spring/summer. Cala is usually two feet tall with grey fur and small bug-like wings that he hides. While he typically adopts more of the look of his Ratkin heritage in public, when exposed to Fey magic he tends to morph more Fey in appearance, dropping the innate glamor that keeps him as ratty as possible.

The preference for keeping his appearance more like his Ratkin side comes from experiences where Fey were mistrusted and disliked for simply existing. He also spent a significant amount of time in full rat form as the familiar of a captive goblin wizard. Indentured to the goblin through the machinations of her owner, a miserable elven archmage, he was known only as Pumpkin then. When a crafty halfling caused him to accidentally care about someone other than himself, the spell was dismantled, returning Pumpkin to his former Calabaza self.

Cala fled the wizard's home, where he'd been deeply loved and unwittingly enslaved all the same. After being rescued from extreme mana sickness by the greatest paladin of the land, he found himself knocking about the City of Lights with just his wits and his bare paws. His memory is patchy and he tends not to dwell on it anymore, assuming it will come back when it cares to. What is memory after all, but the ghost of an experience coming back to haunt you?

The liminal space of shadows and secret places are where the Cala feels most at home. Predisposed to making nests, ferreting out goods that "seem" abandoned, and chewing on leather goods, Cala finds trouble more often than not. Having inherited none of his father's strength and little of his mother's smarts, the Ratkin-Fey relied on a stealthy approach and a limber lightning-like ability to dip and dodge to make his escape. His nose and ears work to keep him safe, relying on rodent cunning and a gregarious disposition to get him through most social situations.

Cala avoids Saba at all costs. Too much emotional turmoil and such. Cala loved her, didn't like being pressganged to Saba as a familiar by her ex husband Victor.
